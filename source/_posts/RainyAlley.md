At first reading, the poem "Rain Alley" sounds to me like describing the poet's beloved and desired girl who passed by â€œlike a lilac spray", but considering the historical background of the poem and referring to some analysis of the poem, I realize that  it is not merely about love and pursuit for a specific girl. Dai Wangshu is using the image of a graceful girl, whom he loves, desires, yet misses the opportunity to get closer to as a personification of his beloved country. When he says "apathetic, sad and disconsolate" and "broke-down bamboo fence", he is referring to his home country who is experiencing hard times. The "moving away" girl also symbolizes the disillusion of his passion and wish. 

I also read a different translated version by Gladys Yang and Xianyi Yang. which I like better. For example, the counterpart of the  line "apathetic, sad, and disconsolate" is "aloof, sad, and melancholy" in Gladys and Xianyi's version.
